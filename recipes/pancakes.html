Grandmother's pancakes

Description

Learn to make pancakes the way grandma used to make them!

Everyone will love it!

Ingredients

  • Milk, semi-skimmed or whole - 250ml
  • 2 Eggs
  • 100g all purpose flower
  • Vanila sugar - 10g
  • Baking powder - 5
  • Baking butter

Steps

  1. Split the eggs and combine them with the milk and vanilla sugar, until you get a homogenous mixture.
  2. Through a sif slowly start adding the flour while continously stirring.
  3. Let the mixture rise for about 10 minutes.
  4. Heat up a pan on medium heat until you can observe the Leidenfrost effect by adding a drop of water.
  5. Take the pan of the heat and add some butter.
  6. Using a ladle, use one scoop of the mixture.
  7. Place the pan back on the heat.
  8. Flip the pancake once it comes loose on it's one while shaking the pan.
  9. Repeat this previous step and then the pancake is ready.
  10. Finish with toppings of your choice, bon appetit!
