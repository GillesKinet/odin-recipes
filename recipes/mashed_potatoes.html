Mashed potatoes

Description

Learn to make mashed potatoes the way grandpa used to make them!

You will love it!

Ingredients

  • Milk, semi-skimmed or whole - one cup
  • 500g soft boiling potatoes
  • Salt
  • White pepper
  • Nutmeg
  • Water

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil.
  2. While the water is getting to a boil, peel the potatoes and slice them in small cubes.
  3. Once the water is boiling add the potatoes and some salt to water as well.
  4. After 20 minutes of boiling, drain the water from the potatoes.
  5. Mash the potatoes and add the milk.
  6. Season with nutmeg, white pepper and salt.
  7. You are all done!
