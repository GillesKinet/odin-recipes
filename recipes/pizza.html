Pizza

Description

Learn all about one of Italians best chefs pizza!

Your family will love it!

Ingredients

  • Water - 100g
  • Patience
  • Flour Tipo 00 - 200g
  • 5g yeast
  • Olive oil - 2 tbsp
  • Fresh basil
  • Fresh burata
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Oregano
  • Garlic
  • Table Salt

Steps

  1. Combine the water and dough and knead for 10 minutes until you get a smooth dough.
  2. Place the dough in a bowl and let it rest for atleast 90 minutes on room temperature, cover the bowl with a wet kitchen towl.
  3. Roll out the dough, place some flour on the kitchen counter to prevent it from sticking.
  4. In a seperate bowl mix your canned tomatoes, add salt, olive oil, one clove of garlic (crushed) and oregano to taste.
  5. Apply a small amount off this mixture to your pizza, just enough so it's covered in sauce, don't cover the edges.
  6. Add your fresh burata, add small pieces, make sure its shredded first.
  7. Place the pizza in pre-heated oven on 250Â°C for about 10 minutes
  8. Take the pizza out and add some fresh basil as toppings and enjoy!
